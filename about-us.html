About Us

Learn about our mission to transform lives across Africa

Our Story

For more than 50 years, the Hisani foundation has supported local, inclusive and positive initiatives in response to needs on the ground in terms of health, education, protection and emergency.

Hisani works to keep children off the streets and from abusive homes and situations. We aim to give them a home and an education. We have about 3.6M Orphans who are unloved and unwanted, so we try everyday to make their lives a little more easy.

Our name "Hisani" means "charity" in Swahili, reflecting our deep connection to the African communities we serve and our commitment to compassionate giving.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable support to vulnerable African communities through education, healthcare, shelter, and emergency aid, empowering individuals to build better futures.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to education, healthcare, and a loving home. We envision thriving African communities free from poverty and filled with opportunity.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, transparency, and community drive everything we do. We believe in sustainable change through partnership and local empowerment.
