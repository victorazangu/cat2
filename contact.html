Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to Hisani?

You can donate through our website, bank transfer, or mobile money (M-Pesa). Contact us for more details on payment methods.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Hisani is a registered non-profit organization. We provide donation receipts for tax purposes upon request.

How do I become a volunteer?

Visit our Volunteer page to learn about opportunities and submit your application.

Can I visit your programs?

Yes! We welcome visitors to see our work firsthand. Please contact us to arrange a visit.

How is my donation used?

85% of donations go directly to our programs. We publish annual reports showing exactly how funds are allocated.

Do you accept corporate partnerships?

Absolutely! We partner with businesses for sponsorships, employee volunteering, and cause marketing. Contact us to discuss.
