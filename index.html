About us

For more than 50 years, the foundation has supported local, inclusive and positive initiatives in response to needs on the ground in terms of health, education, protection and emergency. Hisani works to keep children off the streets and from abusive homes and situations. We aim to give them a home and an education. We have about 3.6M Orphans who are unloved and unwanted, so we try everyday to make their lives a little more easy.
