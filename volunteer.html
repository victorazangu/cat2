Why Volunteer With Us?

At Hisani, volunteers are at the heart of everything we do. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or want to commit to a longer-term project, your time and skills can make a tremendous impact on the lives of vulnerable communities across Africa.

Join thousands of passionate individuals who are already making a difference. Together, we can create lasting change.

Benefits of Volunteering

Flexible Schedule

Choose hours that work for you. We offer various volunteer opportunities to fit any schedule.

Build Connections

Meet like-minded people passionate about making a difference and build lasting friendships.

Gain Experience

Develop new skills and gain valuable experience that can enhance your personal and professional life.

Make an Impact

See the direct results of your efforts as you help transform lives in communities across Africa.

Volunteer Opportunities

Find the perfect way to contribute your time and talents

Teaching & Tutoring

Help children learn by teaching classes or providing one-on-one tutoring in various subjects.

  • Time: 4-8 hours/week
  • Location: On-site or Remote
  • Skills: Teaching experience preferred

Healthcare Support

Assist with health clinics, community health education, and medical supply distribution.

  • Time: 6-10 hours/week
  • Location: On-site
  • Skills: Medical background helpful

Administrative Support

Help with office tasks, data entry, donor communications, and event planning.

  • Time: 2-6 hours/week
  • Location: Remote or On-site
  • Skills: Computer literacy

Fundraising & Events

Help organize fundraising events, campaigns, and community outreach activities.

  • Time: Varies by event
  • Location: Various
  • Skills: Communication skills
